WAR

In this version of war we will have two players who are going to battle. The name of the game is war. the goal of this game is to collect all the cards of the opponent. For this version you will be asked to drag and drop your card to play into the battle box, which after your opponent presents his card the game will compare and add the winners cards to the deck.

    Rules
  • One player goes at a time
  • Lower card loses
  • Winner is who obtains the whole deck
    Sub-Rules
  1. Players must have fun
  2. shoot for victory
  3. No mercy
